Melt is an American band formed in New York City in 2017, consisting of vocalist Veronica Stewart-Frommer, guitarist Marlo Shankweiler and bassist Lucas Saur.

Olivia Lauryn Dean (born 14 March 1999) is a British singer and songwriter. She has released two studio albums, Messy (2023) and The Art of Loving (2025), as well as two live albums and five EPs. Her accolades include a Grammy Award, and nominations for eight Brit Awards and the Mercury Prize.
